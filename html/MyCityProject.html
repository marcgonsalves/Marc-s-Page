City Of Bellevue

Breif Description

Bellevue is located in Washington State across from Lake Washington over in Seattle. It is home to a lot of beautiful areas such as the Botanical Gardens, Downtown Park, Bellevue Arts Museum, Woodland Park, and much more. Here are some of those famous locations.

Famous Locations

Downtown Park

One of Bellevue's famous location is Downtown Park. It is a twenty acre farm that consists of a circular ring with a large central greenspace that is surrounded by a moat and walking trails. There are many picnic areas located inside the park with views of the skyline and Mount Ranier. If you want a nice place to relax and spend time with family, then Downtown Park is the place for you.

Crossroads Mall

Another one of Bellevue's astute locations is the Crossroads Mall. This is my personal favorite location as it was next to my first apartment. I spent my childhood at this mall and trust me when I say, it's a lot more than just a mall. The mall is open every day and has many stores, necessities, and a food court to eat at. Outside of the mall there are two different playgrounds, a basketball park, a water area, and a nice long track to ride your bike or scooter. Your kids could even play chess or visit the library under supervision! I recommend you to visit this mall because it is a great place to waste time or just relax.

Botanical Gardens

The last location in this list is the Botanical Gardens. The Botanical Gardens are located East of Downtown Bellevue. The garden gets 300,000 visits a year due to it's amazing beauty, scents, and wildlife. In winter, they have a special event where they decorate the gardens with LED lights. They make animals out of these lights that kids have to find as a scavenger hunt. It is one of Bellevue's most scenic locations and I recommend you to visit it ASAP!
